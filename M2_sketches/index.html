  • slide: Use the pointer to control the slide
  • change lightness: Use pinch out and slide to change the lightness of the background (touchscreen recommended)
  • pinch to change lightness: Use pinch in and slide to change the lightness of the background (touchscreen recommended)
  • two finger slider: Use two fingers and pinch it to change the lightness of the background (touchscreen required)
  • rotative button: Use two fingers to control the background (touchscreen required)
  • multiple fingers drag: Grab the object with multiple fingers and drag it (touchscreen required)
  • two fingers drag: Grab the object from top (center) with two fingers and drag it (touchscreen required)
  • two fingers drag-2: Grab the object from top (side) with two fingers and drag it (touchscreen required)
